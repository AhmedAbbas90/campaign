Who we are

Our Values

Everything GWC does is grounded in a clear set of values. These are not slogans - they are the standards we hold ourselves to, and the standards you should hold us to.

⚖️

Fairness

Equal treatment, equal opportunity, equal pay. We advocate for rules that apply to everyone - without exception.

🔍

Transparency

We publish what we negotiate. No private deals, no closed-door decisions. You deserve to know everything we know.

🤝

Inclusivity

Every nationality, every role, every level. We represent the whole workforce - not just the loudest voices.

💬

Empathy

We listen before we act. Your experience is the starting point of every initiative we take.

📋

Accountability

We set goals. We report results. If we fall short, we say so. Accountability is not just a word - it's a practice.

🌍

Global Perspective

Our workforce speaks 19+ languages. Our council reflects that. Diverse perspectives make better decisions.
