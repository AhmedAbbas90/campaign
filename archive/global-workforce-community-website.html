🤖 Navigating AI & Future of Work

The rapid integration of AI demands active management. We will negotiate binding agreements on how new technologies are implemented to protect job security and ensure ethical employee data use.

🚀 Fair Internal Mobility & Career Pathing

We demand transparent and fair processes for internal transfers, reskilling programs, and promotions. Career advancement should be based on merit, not network, with a clear path forward.

🛡️ Erosion of Rights & Privacy

Reports of surveillance and retaliation for free expression are alarming. Your digital privacy and right to speak without fear must be uncompromisingly defended.

🗣️ A Passive vs. Proactive Council

A Works Council should be a vocal advocate, not a rubber stamp. We need strong, active representation that challenges unfair policies and truly fights for employee interests.

📝 Accountability in Performance Reviews

Performance reviews must be a tool for growth, and development , not a weapon for pressure. We will ensure the process is fully transparent, legally compliant, and includes a clear channel to challenge biased ratings.

👥 Underrepresented Voices Ignored

One-size-fits-all solutions don't work in a diverse workplace. Without authentic representation from all communities, crucial perspectives are lost and inequalities persist.

Our Mandate for Action: A 10-Point Commitment

This is not a list of promises; it is a reflection of your voices. Built from hundreds of conversations and shared concerns, this is our contract with you.

Our Day-One Priorities

🛡️🎓

Reskilling and upskilling program

We demand a robust reskilling and upskilling program to guarantee future-proofing careers in the AI era and a pathway to a new role. We will ensure technological change serves employees, not replaces them.

🤝

Radical Transparency in Negotiations

We will pull back the curtain on negotiations with management. You deserve to know what’s being decided on your behalf and have a say in the process.

🔐

An Unshakeable Defense of Your Voice

We will fiercely protect your right to express political views and show solidarity, ensuring performance management is never used as a tool for retaliation.

Completing Our 10-Point Mandate

Ending the Black Box of Promotions & Pay

We will demand and enforce transparent, equitable criteria for career advancement and compensation. No more ambiguity, no more bias—just clear, fair pathways for growth.

A Zero-Tolerance Anti-Discrimination Program

Moving beyond policy to action, with mandatory training, community-led enablement, and truly confidential reporting channels free from retaliation.

Holistic Support for You & Your Family

Expanding parental leave, improving visa support, and implementing a compassionate grief support plan to care for families in times of crisis.

Protect & Expand Modern Work

We will defend our existing hybrid and work-from-abroad policies and advocate for even greater flexibility to support a healthy work-life balance.

Prioritize Mental Health & Wellbeing

We will fight for expanded, accessible mental health resources and proactive policies that genuinely support employee wellbeing and prevent burnout.

Empowering All Teams & Recognizing Value

We will ensure a strong voice for Tech, Product, and beyond in all decisions, while building fair recognition programs that truly value your contributions.

Safeguard Your Digital Rights

We will conduct a full audit of all employee monitoring software, challenge surveillance, and protect your privacy from unwarranted intrusion.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about voting, pledging, and the Works Council

What is a Works Council? +

The Works Council (Betriebsrat) is a legally mandated employee representation body in German companies. It negotiates with management on working conditions, changes to employment terms, and ensures employee rights are protected. It's YOUR voice in company decisions.

How does voting work? +

Voting will take place in May 2026 (exact dates TBC by Electoral Board). You can vote by mail or at in-person polling stations. All Zalando SE employees are eligible. Your vote is confidential and protected by law.

Is my vote truly confidential? +

Absolutely. Works Council elections are governed by strict German labor law (BetrVG). Your vote is secret ballot, and there is zero way for management or anyone else to know how you voted. Your job is fully protected.

What does "pledging support" mean? +

A pledge is your non-binding commitment to vote for our list. It helps us plan outreach and mobilization. Your pledge is confidential—we don't share names with management or other employees. The actual vote in May is what counts.
