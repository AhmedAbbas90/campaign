A Works Council That Acts for You

Real Voice. Real Change. Real Accountability.

We know the challenges you face every day: unclear decisions that affect your work, compensation structures that feel opaque, stunted growth opportunities, and too often being the last to know about changes that impact your livelihood. We deserve better. You deserve a Works Council that listens with empathy, acts with transparency, and delivers measurable improvements to your working life and beyond.

Our commitments

1. We will enforce your legal rights 

You deserve to have your legal rights respected. Right now, some employees face unjust performance plans, misleading terminations, harassment, and opaque transfers with little support and even less choice. We are convinced that a strong, educated Works Council can change this. We learn from top experts, use that knowledge to deliver real results in your interest, and keep you informed every step of the way.

2. We will fight for equitable pay 

Equal work deserves equal pay for all. We will advocate for transparent and just salary bands, and clear and just processes for promotions and raises. No more hoping that you're being paid fair. And we'll ensure the process is documented, consistent, and accessible to all employees.

3. We will prioritize your mental health 

These are uncertain times. Uncertainty breeds anxiety. We have all had our fair share of rough patches to understand that deeply. And how it affects your mental health. We believe that your psychological safety is more important than any macroeconomic statistic or internal KPI. We will be there if you have any specific needs. We will press for better flexibility in your work life, protection for your private life, your rights as a caregiver, and your time in general. We believe that the long term mental well being of employees is an essential ingredient for our organization’s well-being.

4. We will bring transparency in your performance evaluation 

Opaque evaluations lead to opaque decisions. The result is a stagnant career. We commit to fixing this. We will advocate for structured and transparent career paths, accessible training programs. Whether you want to deepen expertise or explore new roles, we'll work to ensure the company invests in your professional growth with concrete programs and budgets. When you grow, Zalando grows.


Our Philosophy

1. We are transparent 

No more decisions behind closed doors. We will ensure management consults the Works Council on all matters affecting working conditions; from reorganizations to policy changes. You'll receive clear, timely communication about what's being discussed and why. Every quarter, we'll publish a transparent report on active negotiations and outcomes.

2. We are inclusive 

Every worker's perspective counts. We will create regular channels for feedback through monthly town halls, anonymous suggestion systems, listening sessions specific to a group of employees impacted by a management decision, published summaries of all negotiations with management, and quarterly accountability reports showing what we've achieved and where we've fallen short. Your concerns will shape our priorities. We represent all workers: new hires and veterans, technical and support staff, full-time and part-time employees alike. We will engage with you and consult you on all major decisions during the decision-making process, because only together can we create the change that we all want to see.
