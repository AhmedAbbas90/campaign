It was also from there that I got called to become a ZMS WoCo regular member, and later on got officially appointed as the Lead of the Economics Committee, where I learned and tried to build the EcoCo for ZMS from scratch, including organizing proper trainings with lawyers, setting up committee’s work routines and structures, connecting with P&O on regular data exchange, etc - that rendered me not only the experience of how to run an EcoCo, but also valuable insights into how our company/group is financially functioning. Aside from these data-related expertise, I also have the basic knowledge of the Works Constitution Act and some skills for negotiation.
