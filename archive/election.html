Welcome

Welcome to the Election Hub

This channel serves as the central information point for the 2026 Works Council Election. The Electoral Board has created this space to ensure every employee has equal access to information, forms, and updates throughout the election period.

What you will find on this page:
  • Official Announcements: The "Election Notice" and legal deadlines.
  • Download Center: Forms for candidacy, supporting signatures, and absentee ballots.
  • Transparency: Who represents the Electoral Board and how to contact us.
  • Voter Verification: Information on how to check if you are eligible to vote.
