To our Muslim community family,

## **Stand With Those Who Stand With You**

**Support [Global Workforce Community:](https://zlife.zalando.net/workspaces/global-workforce-community/apps/content/our-mission) A Shield You Can Trust**

We want to be very direct and speak from the heart.

This Works Council Election 2026 gives all of us a choice. There are different lists and different styles of representation. We respect that every colleague will decide what is best for them

But for us, this election comes down to one simple question: **who has truly stood with us?**

We did not step into this because being in the Works Council was a personal goal. In truth, it was never our ambition.

Stepping forward for our colleagues during challenging times came at a real cost. But we stood firm. We did it because we believe our community deserves those who will not look away, stay silent, or back down when things become difficult.

**Why Support GWC:**

This is not just another list. It is a coalition of trusted colleagues, community figures, and leaders from diverse backgrounds across Zalando who have already been showing up for people, defending employee rights, protecting dignity, and pushing back against discrimination long before this election. We came together to ensure that no group is ever marginalized, targeted, or left without strong support again.

### **Your Vote is Your Power**

This is our chance to build a Works Council that truly understands our daily lives and struggles. By standing together, we ensure that our diverse realities remain at the heart of the company’s decisions.

### **Election Reminders**

* **Signatures:** Providing a support signature is **not a binding vote** \- \-you can choose any list you want on the election dates
* **Election Dates:** **5 – 8 May 2026**.
* **Postal Voting:** If you’ll be away, please [request postal voting](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLScSLmTHmPT2LACePMpk2kFPf-Ufr0iLjvy_Rv_s3dx_sruCWA/viewform) from the **Electoral Board** in time.

Thank you for following and supporting the **[Global Workforce Community](https://zlife.zalando.net/workspaces/global-workforce-community/apps/content/who-we-are)**.

We’re in this together. If you want the full picture on our goals for the council, message any of us 1-1. We will make sure you have everything you need to spread the word.

**Your future at work is important. Let’s protect it together.**
